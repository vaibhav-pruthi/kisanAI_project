🌱 About Us

Welcome to our AI Personal Gardening Assistant — your smart companion for cultivating healthy, thriving plants in any setting!

Whether you're managing a personal kitchen garden, running a nursery, overseeing a small-scale farm, or operating a greenhouse, our intelligent assistant is designed to make plant care easier, more efficient, and more informed.

🌿 What We Do

  • 🌾 Plant Disease Detection: Upload an image and our AI identifies potential plant diseases, providing:
    • Disease Name
    • Symptoms
    • Causes
    • Recommended Treatments
  • 💧 Smart Irrigation Prediction: Predicts water requirements based on:
    • Soil Type
    • Soil Moisture
    • Plant Type
    • Environmental Factors

🧠 Behind the Tech

We trained our disease detection model using the trusted PlantVillage dataset and deployed it using EfficientNet, a high-performance deep learning model optimized for accuracy and speed.

Our irrigation prediction system uses a range of features and modeling techniques to help your plants stay hydrated just right.
